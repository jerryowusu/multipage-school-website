Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program UGC/DEB approved?

Yes, we are the world's first online university-approved by the Distance Education Board, a bureau of the University Grants Commission. Please check the link for further information- https://ugc.ac.in/deb

I plan to migrate abroad, will this degree be helpful/recognized?

A degree from school is not just recognized at a national level, but we also have accreditations by WASC -USA AND QAA-UK. Our Degree is recognized and globally accepted and you will not face any problems in case you wish to study abroad/migrate.

What is the passing criteria for this program?

The minimum passing criteria is 4.5 SGPA(semester grade point average) and 5 CGPA (cumulative grade point average)

How to apply for the program?

You can apply through our Online Campus and Enrol Now: https://jerryowusu.com/school/Home/Basic or call on our Toll-Free number +233 248045875 for assistance. Alternatively, you may write into jerryowusu@amity.edu
